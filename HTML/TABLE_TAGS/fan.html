How Does Fan Works?

The first ceiling fan was invented in late 1800s by Philip Diehi. Who use a sewing machine motor to power it. A ceiling fan has motor which rotates with blade.A fan works by converting electrical energy into mechanical energy to create airflow and a cooling effect. The fan's motor, typically an electric motor, rotates the blades, which push air and create a breeze. This breeze helps to evaporate sweat, reducing body heat and providing a cooling sensation.
