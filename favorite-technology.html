My Favorite Technology
    • Automotive technology

    The technology in cars is broadly referred to as automotive technology, vehicle technology, or car technology. More specifically, it can be categorized into areas like driver assistance systems (ADAS), autonomous driving, connected vehicles, and powertrain technology.

  1. Coding

    2. Coding, also known as computer programming, is the process of creating a set of instructions (code) that tells a computer what to do. These instructions are written in a specific programming language, allowing humans to communicate with machines and build various software, applications, and websites.


  2. Agricultural Technology

    3. Agricultural technology or agrotechnology (abbreviated agtech, agritech, AgriTech, or agrotech) is the use of technology in agriculture, horticulture, and aquaculture with the aim of improving yield, efficiency, and profitability. Agricultural technology can be products, services or applications derived from agriculture that improve various input and output processes.


  3. Health Technology

    4. Health technology is defined by the World Health Organization as the "application of organized knowledge and skills in the form of devices, medicines, vaccines, procedures, and systems developed to solve a health problem and improve quality of lives".[1] This includes pharmaceuticals, devices, procedures, and organizational systems used in the healthcare industry,[2] as well as computer-supported information systems.


  4. Headphone

    5. Headphones are a pair of small loudspeaker drivers worn on or around the head over a user's ears. They are electroacoustic transducers, which convert an electrical signal to a corresponding sound. Headphones let a single user listen to an audio source privately, in contrast to a loudspeaker, which emits sound into the open air for anyone nearby to hear. Headphones are also known as earphones[1] or, colloquially, cans.
